Jason J. Calvin (CV)
Email: jasoncalvin@g.harvard.edu

Jason is a postdoctoral fellow at Harvard University working in the research group of Jarad Mason since September 2022. He is working on the development of microporous water, an aqueous system with high gas capacities enabled by colloidal nanoparticles of framework materials. He received his Ph.D. in Chemistry from the University of California, Berkeley advised by Paul Alivisatos in 2022. His research focused on the thermodynamics of the organic ligand shell of colloidal quantum dots. Prior to that, he got his B.S. in Chemistry from Brigham Young University in 2018 and worked in the research group of Brian Woodfield. While there, he studied low-temperature heat capacities of a variety of materials.
